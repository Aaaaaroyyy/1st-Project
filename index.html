About SR Marine

SR Marine is a leading Sales and Service provider for Marine Navigation & Communication Equipments as well as Marine Paints in the Pan India Network. We are the authorized Sales and Service agents in India for a number of leading International Manufacturers of Marine Navigation and Communication Equipments, Like – NEW SUN RISE CO., LTD., CHINA, SAMYUNG ENC CO., LTD., KOREA, NANTONG SAIYANG ELECTRONICS CO., LTD., CHINA, SHENZHEN SHUNHANG NAVIGATION TECH CO., LTD., CHINA and also authorized agent for EXCEL INDIA PROTECTIVE PAINTS PVT., LTD., MUMBAI.

SR Marine used to market the product and provide uninterrupted quality services to the clients. Since inception the company has shown remarkable progress and performances to the valued clients of ours.

services we provide

SR Marine provides 24x7 onboard services and support for all Navigational & Communication Equipment’s. Presently we have our service presence at Kolkata, Port Blair and Mumbai respectively and for rest all the ports in PAN India network our technical team is always ready to reach out to the customers, as desired.

  • VDR/SVDR Inspections and Annual Performance Tests all the Ports across the country
  • Installation of Equipment’s
  • Commissioning of Equipment’s
  • After Sales Service Support
